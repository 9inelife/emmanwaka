Establish Good Governance

I shall run an inclusive and accountable government with sincerity of purpose and transparency to ensure the prosperity of the state across all areas.

Foster Patriotism

I will develop activities and create a value system that builds love, allegiance and commitment in the people towards the State.

Provide Quality Education

I will ensure that quality education truly becomes the bedrock of economic development in the state by providing adequate infrastructure, investing in teacher competence, designing effective curriculums, and creating a system that gives access to affordable education for all.

Ensure Security

I will ensure that the people feel safe by strengthening, and adequately equipping and training relevant security agencies.

Boost Commerce and Trade

I shall harness the acclaimed productive and creative capacity of Abians to enhance the competitiveness of the state, boost its economy and make it assume its pride of place among South Eastern states and beyond.
